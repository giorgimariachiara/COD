Analyzing the Number of Women in Political Parties
Discovering Which Parties Have the Highest Representation of Women and Their Political Alignments
Casting a Gendered Spotlight: Exploring Women's Representation Across Italian Political Parties

How many women are represented in each political party, and is political alignment a significant factor in this regard? In our ongoing exploration of the Italian political landscape, the fourth research question delves into the gender composition of political parties and their alignment within the broader spectrum. Understanding the relationship between political alignment and gender representation can shed light on the dynamics of gender inclusivity within the Italian political landscape. It is within this context that we embark on our analysis, aiming to uncover patterns, trends, and disparities that contribute to a more comprehensive understanding of the state of gender equality in Italian politics.

    An Overview ordered of Parties by Female Representation
  • Trasversalismo (Transversalism):
    Leading with 41% female representation, "MOVIMENTO 5 STELLE" reflects a commitment to gender diversity. What is important to point out is that, in our data-base the MS5 party is the only one with transversalism alignment.
  • Sinistra (Left):
    Parties in this category exhibit an average of 21% female representation.
    "LIBERI E UGUALI" demonstrates 30% female representation, indicating a strong presence of women in left-wing politics.
  • Centro-destra (Center-right):
    These parties have an average of 18% female representation. "CORAGGIO ITALIA" leads with 35% female participation, reflecting a noteworthy effort to engage women in center-right politics.
  • Estrema sinistra (Extreme Left):
    Parties on the extreme left exhibit an average of 16% female representation. "PARTITO COMUNISTA ITALIANO" leads with 15% female participation, suggesting a commitment to gender diversity in the far-left political spectrum.

  • Centro-sinistra (Center-left):
    These parties have an average of 14.2% female representation. "PARTITO DEMOCRATICO" ranks highest with 34% female participation, indicating a substantial presence of women in center-left politics.
  • Centro (Center): Parties in the center exhibit an average of 11.71% female representation. "ITALIA DEI VALORI" showcases 8% female participation, while "SCELTA CIVICA" follows with 19%, indicating a diverse range of female engagement within centrist parties.
  • Destra (Right): Right-wing parties have an average of 9.78% female representation. "FRATELLI D'ITALIA" stands out with 29% female participation, showcasing a notable effort to involve women in right-wing politics.
  • Estrema destra (Extreme Right): "MOVIMENTO SOCIALE ITALIANO - DESTRA NAZIONALE" ranks highest in this category with 3% female representation, indicating a significant gender disparity within extreme right-wing movements. It is important, in this particular case, to poin out that this party is the only one to be part of the Extreme Right.
